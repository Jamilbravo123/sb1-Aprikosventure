## 8. Sources and Methodology

All market sizing uses top-down TAM → SAM → SOM methodology:
- **TAM:** Total addressable market based on industry reports (BCG, McKinsey, Chainalysis)
- **SAM:** Serviceable addressable market based on geographic, regulatory, and segment focus
- **SOM:** Serviceable obtainable market based on NDAP's specific capacity and projections

Data sources: Chainalysis Global Crypto Adoption Index, BCG RWA Tokenization Report, UNCTAD Pakistan Trade Data, Statista, CoinMarketCap, World Bank Remittance Data.

*Note: All figures are estimates and will be updated with current data as research progresses.*
